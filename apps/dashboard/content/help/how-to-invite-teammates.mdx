On 7qr, you can easily invite your teammates to join your 7qr project. This is useful if you want to collaborate with your team.

## Sending an invite

If you haven't already, [create a project](what-is-a-project#how-to-create-a-project) on 7qr.

To invite a teammate:

1. Navigate to your project's Members Settings page. You can do that by clicking on the **Settings** link in the menu bar at the top of your project dashboard, and click on the **People** tab on the left sidebar.
2. Click on the **Invite** button to open the Invite Teammate modal.
3. Enter your teammate's email address and click on the **Send invite** button.

## Revoking / resending an invite

If you want to revoke / resend an invitation, you can do so by following these steps:

1. Navigate to your project's Members Settings page.
2. CLick on the **Invitations** tab.
3. Click on the `⋮` button next to the invitation you want to revoke.
4. Click on the **Remove** button to open the Remove Invitation modal.
5. Click on the **Confirm remove** button to confirm.

## Removing a teammate

If a teammate no longer needs access to your project, you can remove them from your project.

1. Navigate to your project's Members Settings page.
2. Click on the `⋮` button next to the teammate you want to remove.
3. Click on the **Remove** button to open the Remove Teammate modal.
4. Click on the **Confirm remove** button to confirm.

## How many teammates can I invite?

7qr has the most generous free plan in the industry. You can invite up to 2 teammates to your project (3 total users) on the free plan.

Need to invite more teammates? Upgrade to our [Pro plan](/pricing) for unlimited teammates.
